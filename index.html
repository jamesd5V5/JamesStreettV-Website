About Me

I'm James Streett the Fifth, a recent and eager UC San Diego graduate. My work lives at the intersection of technology and creativity, where audio engineering, machine learning, and software development come together to push perspectives.

As an Audio Engineer at UCSD, I mixed and archived over 90 live performances ranging from orchestral concerts to experimental theater. I also founded Mammoth Plugins, a startup for games that has delivered 15+ custom plugins for titles like Minecraft and Stardew Valley. Outside of work, I’ve competed in Game Jam competitions, creating full game soundtracks and SFX under 96-hour deadlines, with multiple top 10% finishes. Most recently as of Summer 2025, I interned at VoiceTune AI in Dublin, where I finetuned LLM-based tools for generative audio and voice modulation.

Fluent in Java, Python, and C#, and experienced with Pro Tools, Ableton Live, and audio DSP, I’m passionate about developing AI audio tools to expand analysis but still keep music creative. I always love to challenge myself with new projects and learn from collaborations, whether it be for software or music.
